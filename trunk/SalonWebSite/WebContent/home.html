Threading is a way carried out by expert beauticians to achieve professional looking results using cotton to thread away and remove unwanted facial hair. This vedio show how fast and easy way our Estheticians remove the eyebrows to make our customer looks attractive.

First of all we cleanse the area, use a pre-wax lotion, makeup remover wipes, a soap and water to just remove any makeup or residue from the area. Finally, We use cotton to remove unwanted facial hair.


Eyebrow Shaping

Threading can add precise shape and definition to the eyebrows by taking off as much or as little hair as you prefer, notably raising the arch of the eyebrow and eliminating the uni-brow while maintaining the full length of the brows.

Threading Environment

In a peaceful environment, the skilled technicians understand the shape of your face and your brow which results in a very perfect eyebrow shape. We just don't clean up the eyebrows instead we shape them accordingly, if necessary. Clients choice takes the first priority.

Benefits of threading

One of the greatest benefits of threading is that is less messier and faster as compared to waxing and tweezing for hair removal, especially hair on the upper lip and eyebrows and also a lot more effective and less painful.Threading helps slow down hair re-growth.
