About Us

THE CLASSIC EYEBROWS provides excellent service to the clients in the form of less painful threading and precise eyebrow shapes.

Experienced and skilled self employed owners take their time to present you with well shaped even eyebrows. Clients choice takes the first priority, as we listen carefully to your needs and requests. The technicians explain what is required to fix the shape if needed, helping their client to achieve the best eyebrows they ever had.

THE CLASSIC EYEBROWS do eyebrows for both men and women, upper lip, chin, cheeks, sideburns and neck for women. We'll shaped eyebrows give neatness, expression and a balance to the face.

Why Threading?

  • Threading is more precise and the hair grows back finer and slower. There is no burning, peeling or irritation, and a great remedy for ingrown hair.
  • Facial threading is good alternative to waxing and tweezing, especially for sensitive skin people who are on Retin A and accutane.
  • Eyebrow threading not only removes unwanted hair around your eyebrow but also helps to get a precise and defined eyebrow shape according to your facial features.
  • Most of all, threading exfoliates the skin, giving you a mini facial while the hair is being removed.
